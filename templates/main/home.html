Message from the Archbishop

I am glad to note that the Family Welfare Centre has revamped the marriage bureau website with a new name marryacatholic.com and with special features to suit the needs of the present day.
The present website accommodates divorced, annulled, widowed and specially abled too. This website enables the young people to find suitable catholic partners for marriage. Its main aim is to reach this facility to a wider spectrum of Catholics. It provides options for online registration and online search. The registered people are not only given to register at an affordable subscriptions, but also are provided facilities at the centre to "meet and greet" families whose profiles are appealing. I thank the Director of the Family Welfare Centre and all his collaborators who worked hard in the planning and the implementation of this commendable project.May Almighty God and the Holy Family bless this effort and make it fruitful.

Most. Rev. Dr. Peter Machado Archbishop of Bangalore

Rev. Dr. Peter Machado
(Archbishop of Bangalore)
