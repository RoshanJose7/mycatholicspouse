Thanks for showing interest in registering with marryacatholic.com.

The documents that you would need for registration are:
  1. Your Parish Priest's reference letter (should not be older than 3 months)
  2. Baptism Certificate from your parish (should not be older than 3 months)
  3. Your Highest Educational Qualification Certificate.
  4. We accept 5 photos to be displayed on your profile.
    1. Passport size photo (A close-up of your face)
    2. Your family photo (along with parents and siblings)
    3. 3 other free style photos

All photos must be of modesty and standard, we may reject your application if the photos are found to be inappropriate.
Your photos must be under 2MB in file size.
Photos are accepted only in .jpg format.
Photo dimensions must be of acceptable size, very big photos will fail to get uploaded.

If you have any questions before you start the application, please write to fwcbangalore@gmail.com
